Cleanser

A cleanser is a skincare product used to remove dirt, oil, makeup, and impurities from the skin’s surface.

What it does

It helps keep the skin clean, prevents clogged pores, and prepares the skin to absorb other products like serums and moisturizers.

Ingredients to Look for Based on Skin Type

Skin Type Recommended Ingredients Why These Ingredients?
Oily Salicylic Acid, Tea Tree Oil, Niacinamide Control excess oil, unclog pores
Dry Hyaluronic Acid, Glycerin, Ceramides Hydrate and strengthen skin barrier
Sensitive Aloe Vera, Chamomile, Centella Asiatica Calm irritation and redness
Combination Niacinamide, Green Tea Extract, Lactic Acid Balance oil production and gentle exfoliation
Acne-Prone Benzoyl Peroxide, Salicylic Acid, Sulfur Fight acne-causing bacteria and clear pores

Know About Active Ingredients

Salicylic Acid

A beta-hydroxy acid (BHA) that exfoliates inside the pores to remove dead skin cells and clear acne. Best for oily and acne-prone skin.

Hyaluronic Acid

A powerful hydrator that binds water to skin cells, keeping the skin plump and moisturized. Great for dry and dehydrated skin.

Niacinamide

A form of Vitamin B3 that strengthens the skin barrier, reduces redness, and controls oil production. Suitable for all skin types.
